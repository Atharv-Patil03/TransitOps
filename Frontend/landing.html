Integrated Telematics Modules

A unified suite built to enforce mandatory business safety rules and streamline operations.

Vehicle Registry CRUD

Maintain unique registration numbers, load capacities, odometers, acquisition costs, and real-time status values (Available, On Trip, In Shop, Retired).

Driver Compliance

Monitor license validity, expiration alerts, and safety scores. Automatically prevent suspended drivers from dispatch assignments.

Automated Trip Dispatch

Select routes, vehicles, and drivers. The system automatically validates load capacities and availability before updating status to "On Trip".

Maintenance Workflow

Register oil changes, engine checks, and repairs. Placing a vehicle in the maintenance log automatically marks it "In Shop" and hides it from dispatch pools.

Fuel & Expense Logging

Track fuel usage (liters, costs) and extra transit expenditures. TransitOps automatically calculates total operational costs and vehicle ROI metrics.

Reports & CSV Export

Export operational summaries, fuel efficiencies, and fleet utilization logs directly to CSV for external accounting integrations.
